

You continue down the hallway.

As you walk in the decrepid halls, you notice the halls begin to twist.

The floor seems to become the left wall, while the ceiling becomes the right wall.

Confused, you press on, thinking you can just stay on the path until the right wall becomes your new ground.

But it doesn't.

As you walk, you stay planted to the ground, even while the world seems to twist around you.

Normally, you would question this heavily, but on this particular day, you can't be bothered.
