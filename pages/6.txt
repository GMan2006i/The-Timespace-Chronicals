

Suddenly, while staring at the anomaly and thinking you might be insane, you hear a faint cracking sound.

The ground below you begins to rumble and crack.


You think, you may be going down.

The ground below opens, swallowing you whole, and as you fall for what feels like hours, you realize, that you are probably going to die.
